Company Profile

Who are we?

Elmek Solutions is a multidisciplinary MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, and Plumbing) engineering consultancy.

With nearly two decades of experience across India and the Middle East, we help complex building projects run smoothly and reliably, from design through completion.

We've worked on major infrastructure projects, super‑speciality hospitals, commercial towers, hotels, residential communities, and educational institutions — always with a focus on compliance, sustainability, and cost efficiency.
